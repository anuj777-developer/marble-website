Our Heritage

Mastering the Stone Since 1990

At Shri Shri Rana Marble Handicrafts Handicrafts, we believe that every stone has a soul waiting to be unveiled. For over three decades, our master artisans have dedicated their lives to the ancient craft of marble sculpting, transforming raw, natural stone into breathtaking monuments and luxury decor.

We focus on unparalleled quality and deep devotion in every chisel strike. Every piece that leaves our workshop is a testament to purity, dedication, and timeless artistry.

Sourcing the Best

Premium Marble Slabs

We meticulously craft all our exquisite sculptures using two of the world's most revered materials. We offer both pristine pure white and intricately painted polychrome finishes.

Pure White Makrana Marble Block

The pride of Rajasthan, famously used for the Taj Mahal. Known for its rich heritage and incredible durability that lasts centuries. Perfect for highly detailed, traditional idols.

Pure Vietnam Marble

Renowned globally for its flawless, crystal-clear white purity and incredibly smooth texture. Vietnam marble is the ultimate choice for modern, serene, and pristine unpainted sculptures.
